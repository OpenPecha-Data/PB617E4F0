Brief Offering Prayer to Durtrö Lhamo, Mistress of the Charnel Ground
by the Fifth Dzogchen Rinpoche

jo, durtrö lhamo nöjin ngen nema
Bhyo! Mistress of the charnel ground, yakṣiṇī, mother Ngen Nema,
mingsing gyé sok khor dang chepa nam
Your eight brothers and sisters, and all your retinues,
tündzé döyön gyatsö chötrin di
With this ocean-like cloud of offerings so pleasing to you,
tukdam kang la kornor meltsé dzö
With all your wishes fulfilled, keep watch over our property and prosperity!

By the Fifth Dzogchen Rinpoche, Thupten Chökyi Dorje.
